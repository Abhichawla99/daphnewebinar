## How to Update Images

### To change the hero intro photo:
1. Replace `daphne-hero.jpg` with your new image
2. Or update line 735 in the HTML:
```html
<img src="own-your-healing-assets/images/YOUR-NEW-IMAGE.jpg" alt="Daphne">
```

### To change the about section photo:
1. Replace `daphne-profile.jpg` with your new image

## Image Specifications

### Hero Intro Photo
- Format: Square or portrait
- Recommended size: 600x600px minimum
- Displays as: Circle (160px diameter)
- Style: Warm, approachable, smiling
- File size: Under 300KB

### About Section Photo
- Format: Square preferred
- Recommended size: 600x600px minimum
- Displays as: Square with rounded corners (200x200px)
- Style: Professional, confident
- File size: Under 400KB

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Hero photo:** Choose a warm, smiling photo that creates instant connection
2. **About photo:** Choose a professional photo that establishes expertise
3. **Lighting:** Ensure good lighting in both photos
4. **Background:** Clean backgrounds work best
5. **File size:** Keep images under 500KB for fast page loading
